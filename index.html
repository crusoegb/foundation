About Me

"God has blessed me so much in my life that it's only right that I give back as much as I can."

My name is Crusoe Gongbay, and I am the CEO and Founder of the Crusoe Gongbay Foundation. I was born in Rockville, Maryland, and attended the University of New Mexico where I played football and majored in business. I am the youngest of six children, and my roots are from Monrovia, Liberia, Africa. This foundation is built on faith and the belief in helping those in need.
